FAQ

Questions you might have.

Will this void my warranty?

No. SlapMyMac only reads sensors — it doesn't modify anything. But we are not responsible for what happens if you slap too hard. Slap responsibly.

Does it work on Intel Macs?

No. The accelerometer and lid angle sensor are only available on Apple Silicon MacBooks (M1, M2, M3, M4 and later).

What about battery life?

Minimal impact. The sensor polling is lightweight. When lid sounds are off, it drops to 30Hz. When paused, near zero.

Does it trigger while typing?

No. The detection threshold filters out keyboard vibrations. Only actual taps, slaps, and intentional impacts trigger sounds.

Can I add my own sounds?

Not yet — but it's on the roadmap. Custom sound pack import is planned for a future update.

Can I get a refund?

Yes. 14-day full refund, no questions asked. Email hello@cepalabs.com.

What about lid sounds — do I need to slap for those?

No! Lid sounds respond to screen movement. Open the lid = creak or engine start. Close it = shutdown. Move it back and forth = play instruments. No slapping required.
