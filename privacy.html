Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

What We Collect

SlapMyMac does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app runs entirely on your Mac.

Sensor Data

SlapMyMac reads your MacBook's accelerometer and lid angle sensor to detect slaps and lid movement. This data is processed locally in real-time and is never stored, logged, or sent anywhere.

License Validation

When you activate a license key, the app sends the key to Polar.sh to verify it. No other data is transmitted. Polar.sh's privacy policy applies to the purchase transaction.

Analytics

We do not use any analytics, tracking, or telemetry in SlapMyMac.

Settings

Your preferences (sound pack, sensitivity, cooldown) are stored locally on your Mac using macOS UserDefaults. They never leave your device.
