FAQ

Answers, before you ask.

Will this void my warranty? +

Nope. SlapMyMac only reads sensors — doesn't modify anything. That said, if you slap hard enough to break something, that's between you and AppleCare.

Does it work on Intel Macs? +

No. The accelerometer only exists in Apple Silicon MacBooks (M1 and later). Intel Macs simply don't have the hardware. Sorry, grandpa.

What about battery life? +

Barely noticeable. Sensor polling is lightweight. When lid sounds are off it drops to 30Hz, when paused it's basically zero.

Does it trigger while typing? +

No. The detection filters out keyboard vibrations. Only actual taps, slaps, and intentional hits trigger sounds. Your Slack messages are safe.

Can I add my own sounds? +

Not yet — but it's on the roadmap. Custom sound pack import is planned for a future update.

What's the deal with lid sounds? Do I need to slap for those? +

No slapping needed! Lid sounds respond purely to screen movement. Open → creak. Close → engine off. Back and forth → music. It's a whole separate thing.
